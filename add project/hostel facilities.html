Hostel Facilities at Our College

Accommodation

Our college provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls, with spacious and comfortable rooms to ensure a homely environment.

  • Fully furnished rooms with beds, study tables, and wardrobes.
  • Options for single, double, or triple occupancy rooms.
  • 24/7 electricity and water supply.

Dining Facilities

The hostel mess provides nutritious and hygienic meals catering to diverse tastes.

  • Breakfast, lunch, evening snacks, and dinner.
  • Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options available.
  • Special meals on festivals and celebrations.

Recreational Amenities

We believe in a balance between academics and recreation. The hostel offers:

  • Indoor games like table tennis, chess, and carrom.
  • Gym and fitness center facilities.
  • Common room with television and reading materials.

Security and Medical Support

Ensuring the safety and well-being of students is our top priority.

  • 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance.
  • Resident warden and support staff available round the clock.
  • First-aid and tie-ups with nearby hospitals for emergencies.
